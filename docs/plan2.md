# plan2.md — Contact section structure

## Task / Feature

Planning the structure and content of the Contact section
without using a traditional form.

## Phase 1 — Prompt to AI

"I need a contact section for a portfolio website
without a contact form.
It should be simple, accessible and professional.
Can you propose content structure and layout ideas?"

## Phase 1 — AI Response (Plan)

The AI suggested:

- A minimal contact section with email and social links
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Accessible links and readable text
- Keeping the section visually consistent with the rest of the site

## Phase 2 — Implementation Notes

I implemented:

- A simple contact section with text and links
- No form or JavaScript validation
- Layout and styling adapted manually to my design system
